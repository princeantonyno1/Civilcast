Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a request?
To submit a request, navigate to the 'Requests' section from the main menu and click on the 'New Request' button. Fill out the required fields in the form and click 'Submit'. Your request will then be logged in the system.

What are departments and spaces?
Departments represent the major functional areas within your organization. Spaces are sub-sections within departments used for specific projects, teams, or topics, allowing for more granular organization and collaboration.

How do I manage my account settings?
You can manage your account settings by clicking on your profile avatar in the top-right corner and selecting 'Account Settings'. From there, you can update your personal information, change your password, and manage notification preferences.
